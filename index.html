Our Story

Eliz Bookkeeping was founded by Elizabeth, a chartered bookkeeper who saw a gap in the market for reliable, modern bookkeeping services that small and medium businesses could actually afford.

What started as a one-woman operation in a co-working space in Clerkenwell has grown into a dedicated team of qualified bookkeepers, serving over 250 clients across London and the wider UK.

We believe that great bookkeeping isn't just about crunching numbers — it's about giving business owners the clarity and confidence to make better decisions. Every spreadsheet, every reconciliation, every VAT return is handled with the same care and attention to detail.
